What lives inside
i.
Free Events

Live richly, for free. Free Pilates, pickleball, matcha pop-ups, drinks, food, philosophy clubs & more — shared by your Circle so you can actually go together, not just save the link.

ii.
The Exchange

ETFs, index funds, crypto, DeFi, stock picks, credit scores, budgeting basics — real talk from members who've been exactly where you are. Just the knowledge you should have had years ago.

Your Circle

A small group of people at your stage — sharing wins, asking real questions, and growing wealth together. Plus the option to get matched with a mentor who's already made the moves you're working toward.

How it works

Three moves.
Starting now.

01
Join your Circle

Get matched with a small group of people at your stage. Optionally get paired with a mentor who's already made the moves you're working toward. Your Circle is the foundation — every move starts here.

02
Browse Free Events

Browse what your Circle shared today — free Pilates, matcha pop-ups, events, shared resources. Show up together. Every find is a dollar you didn't spend, and a reason to actually leave the apartment.

03
Turn savings into investments

Ask anything in The Exchange — ETFs, crypto, DeFi, stock picks, credit, budgeting. Learn from people who've already made the move. When you're ready, your Circle holds you to it.
